OVERSIGHT

At Izwe Livumile, we are dedicated to upholding a culture of safety, quality, and reliability. Our team of experienced and hands-on professionals brings decades of expertise from major construction projects across Africa and beyond.

Our services encompass the full spectrum of construction activities and are organized into three main divisions: Building Construction, Civil Engineering, and Roads and Earthworks.

Izwe Livumile stands out with its exceptional service delivery, extending its high-quality construction solutions across the nation. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, the company consistently surpasses expectations and delivers unparalleled results, making a significant impact on every project it undertakes.

Izwe Livumile excels in providing comprehensive construction services that cater to a wide range of needs. Their expertise spans various facets of the industry, ensuring each project benefits from a meticulous and innovative approach that drives exceptional outcomes and client satisfaction.

Why Izwe Livumile is Your First Choice Construction Partner.

  • Our strong culture of professionalism and respect
  • Our responsive senior management who have extensive industry experience
  • Our conservative approach to risk-taking outside of established construction standards
  • Our capability to pursue opportunities across numerous geographies
  • Our broad technical and sector specialisation
  • Our ability to consistently execute and deliver both small and large-scale projects

Izwe Livumile is a trusted construction partner, delivering top-quality results with a focus on client needs and industry standards. Their expertise and craftsmanship make them the preferred choice for exceptional construction services.

THE IZWE LIVUMILE WAY

REPUTATION

Our reputation is one of our greatest assets. We actively work to build a reputation of excellence in our people, systems and products. If our actions in any way contravene our shared values of teamwork, communication, integrity and excellence, it can adversely impact on our positive reputation.

TEAMWORK

We are a unified team at Izwe Livumile. Our success is built on collaboration and mutual support. When a colleague needs assistance, we are there to help, and when we require support, we reach out without hesitation. We believe in lifting each other up and never ignore a colleague in need, even if they haven't asked for help. We are also committed to participating in activities and projects that elevate and promote Izwe Livumile.

SAFETY

At Izwe Livumile, ensuring that everyone on our sites and in our offices returns home safely every day is a fundamental priority. We recognize the profound impact that severe injuries and fatalities can have on our staff, their families, and colleagues. Therefore, beyond developing comprehensive safety policies and procedures, we have cultivated a deep-rooted culture of safety awareness and care for one another. Safety is a daily conversation at Izwe Livumile, embedded in every aspect of our operations, from our reputation and quality to cost awareness, teamwork, and culture. We believe that a safe site is a productive site, making safety an integral part of our business practices. Managed at the highest level within the company, we have implemented specific initiatives to keep our people's safety at the forefront of everything we do.

QUALITY

Quality is of utmost importance to the group and both management and employees must pursue this at all times. Performance of duties should be at the expected level of skill, knowledge, experience and judgement.

CULTURE

We have a positive, “can do” attitude and always “go the extra mile” for our clients. We are flexible, dependable, hardworking and a “pleasure to do business with”.

COST AWARENESS

Management and employees have a responsibility to consider efficiencies where possible, such as cost cutting, value engineering and any other methods that provide our stakeholders with quality structures within set budgets. This responsibility includes taking due care, avoiding waste, and minimising the misuse or abuse of company assets, while limiting the risks to suppliers and subcontractors who engage in projects for the group.
